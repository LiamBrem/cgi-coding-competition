Predict the Winner

Based on: “Predict the Winner” (but simplified for contest clarity)

Problem Statement

Two players are playing a game with a list of integers.
They take turns picking a number from either the leftmost or rightmost end of the list.

Each player plays optimally to maximize their total score.

Determine whether Player 1 can win or tie.

Return:

"1" if Player 1 can win or tie

"0" otherwise

Input Format (stdin)

A single line of space-separated integers.

Example:

1 5 2

Output Format (stdout)

Print:

1


or
